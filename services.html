Finished Products

Finished product testing is an essential process in the pharmaceutical industry to ensure that the final products are safe, effective, and meet the required quality standards. At Roorkee Research & Analytical Labs Pvt Ltd, we conduct comprehensive testing of finished pharmaceutical products to guarantee that they meet regulatory requirements, perform as intended, and maintain the highest levels of quality and safety.

Microbiology

Microbiology testing plays a critical role in ensuring that pharmaceutical products, including raw materials, intermediates, and finished goods, are free from harmful microbial contaminants. At Roorkee Research & Analytical Labs Pvt Ltd, we conduct rigorous microbiological testing to meet regulatory requirements and to ensure the safety, efficacy, and quality of pharmaceutical products.

Stability Testing

Stability testing is crucial in the pharmaceutical industry to determine how a drug product maintains its quality, safety, and efficacy over time when exposed to various environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, and light. At Roorkee Research & Analytical Labs Pvt Ltd, we conduct comprehensive stability testing to ensure that pharmaceutical products retain their desired characteristics throughout their shelf life and storage conditions.
